Our Story

Harikripa Science
School

Harikripa Science School was founded in 2010 with a singular vision — to bring rigorous, values-based science education to the students of Laxmangarh, Sikar and the surrounding region of Rajasthan.

"Every child from Laxmangarh deserves the same quality of education as any student in a big city. That belief built this school."

Located on the Jaipur–Bikaner Highway, Near Police Thana, our school is a co-educational private institution affiliated to the Rajasthan State Board (RBSE). We offer Hindi Medium and English Medium education from Class 1 through Class 12.

Our Science stream at Class 11 and 12 — with Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics — is purpose-built for students aiming for NEET, JEE, and other national-level examinations.

Both full residential boarding and day boarding options are available, making Harikripa Science School accessible to families across Sikar district and beyond.

Our Mission
To provide accessible, high-quality science education rooted in Indian values, enabling every student from Laxmangarh to achieve their fullest potential.
Our Vision
To become Rajasthan's most trusted Science school — where academic excellence and cultural identity grow together.
